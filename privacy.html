Privacy Policy

This page explains how enquiry data is handled for scaffolding quote requests in Portsmouth and surrounding areas.

What we collect

Name, phone number, postcode, optional email, project details, optional uploaded images, and submission metadata (time, referrer and campaign parameters).

Why we collect it

To assess whether a job is serviceable, provide a quote for erected scaffolding, and arrange follow-up related to that enquiry.

How data is shared

Enquiries are sent directly to the appointed scaffolding business and/or its automation systems (for example Make or Zapier webhook routing). Data is not sold to unrelated third parties.

Retention

Data is kept only as long as reasonably needed for quoting, record-keeping, and legal or accounting obligations.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data where applicable under UK data protection law.
